I treat performance as a product feature, not a final polish pass. To keep myself honest, I run with explicit budgets.

## Current budget

1. HTML + CSS + JS total under 170 KB compressed.
2. Hero image under 160 KB when possible.
3. Largest Contentful Paint target under 2.2s on mid-tier mobile.

## Practical habits that help

- Prefer static rendering over unnecessary client JavaScript.
- Compress and resize images before commit.
- Use one clear font family, not many webfont requests.
- Keep animations lightweight and non-blocking.

## Review cadence

Every few posts, I run Lighthouse and compare against this budget. If numbers drift, I fix root causes before adding new visual features.

Small routines are easier to maintain than heroic optimization sprints.
