Founded and led PSRC's Puget Sound Trends equity data program, building public-facing dashboards that track demographic, economic, and transportation indicators across communities in the 4-county region. The program was designed to be repeatable and maintainable, giving agency staff a reliable framework for answering equity questions over time rather than on a project-by-project basis.
